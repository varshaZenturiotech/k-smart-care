CIRCULAR NO: LSGD/HR/2024/112
SUBJECT: Revised Leave Policy for Local Self Government Employees

1. PURPOSE
This circular revises the casual leave and earned leave entitlements for all
Panchayat, Municipality, and Corporation employees under the K-SMART ecosystem.

2. CASUAL LEAVE
Every employee is entitled to 12 days of casual leave per calendar year.
Casual leave cannot be carried forward to the next year. A maximum of 3
consecutive casual leave days may be availed without prior written approval
from the Department Head, provided the employee's dashboard is marked
accordingly at least 24 hours in advance.

3. EARNED LEAVE
Earned leave accrues at the rate of 2.5 days per month of service, subject to
a maximum accumulation of 300 days. Earned leave may be encashed at the time
of retirement or resignation, subject to Finance Department norms.

4. SICK LEAVE
Sick leave of up to 20 days per year is granted on production of a medical
certificate for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days. Sick leave not availed
in a year lapses and cannot be carried forward.

5. SPECIAL DISASTER DUTY LEAVE
Employees deployed for disaster response duty (flood, landslide, or public
health emergency) are entitled to compensatory leave equal to the number of
days served on such duty, to be availed within 90 days of the duty period
ending.

6. APPLICATION PROCEDURE
All leave applications must be submitted through the employee dashboard at
least 3 working days in advance, except in cases of medical emergency. The
reporting officer must approve or reject the application within 48 hours,
after which it is deemed auto-approved.

7. EFFECTIVE DATE
This circular is effective from 1st April 2024 and supersedes Circular
LSGD/HR/2021/087.

Issued by: Directorate of Local Self Government Department, Government of Kerala
